Examination Regulations
  1. Student shall be at the examination room at least fifteen (15) minutes before the time appointed for the commencement of the examination. Candidates should, therefore, refrain from studying in halls earmarked for examinations.
  2. Students must NOT take into the examination hall any programmed electronic device, handset, book, paper, or any other form of printed or written material, hand bags and brief cases.
  3. Student must bring his/her examination room and college ID card to the examination hall and display them on his/her desk.
  4. While examination is in progress, communication between candidates is strictly forbidden, and any candidate found talking or receiving/giving illegal assistance may be required to withdraw from the examination.
  5. Student must sign in the attendance form with his/her Reg. number and must sign out on submission of his/her script at the end of the examination.
  6. Student must correctly fill in all the spaces provided on the cover of the answer booklet and all additional sheets of paper issued during the examination.
  7. No candidate will be permitted to: (a) Enter examination room 30 minutes after the commencement of the examination; (b) Leave the examination room before the first 45 minutes of the examination; (c) Write examination without college ID Card.
  8. Examination answer scripts/sheets whether used or unused should not be taken out of the examination hall by students.
  9. A student involved in examination misconduct or malpractice may be expelled from the College.
  10. Candidates must adhere strictly to the sitting arrangement made by the Chief Invigilator.
